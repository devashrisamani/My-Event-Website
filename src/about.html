Organised by Family, Friends and Community!

The Bayside Heights Residences Craft Market Event is more than a gathering of artists and craft enthusiasts; it is a manifestation of love, respect, and empowerment organized by those closest to the residents of Bayside Heights Residences - their family, friends, and community members.

Who We Are

We are a passionate not-for-profit group of volunteers united by a shared belief in the importance of recognizing and nurturing the independence, creativity, and valuable contributions of our seniors. Comprised of family members, friends, and fellow community members, our team understands the essence of the Bayside Heights community and is dedicated to creating platforms where our older citizens can shine.

Our Mission

Our mission extends beyond organizing a craft market. We aim to celebrate the wisdom, skills, and artistry of the seniors at Bayside Heights Residences and local artists, presenting them not as beneficiaries, but as the very heart and soul of our community.
